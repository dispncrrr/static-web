FAQ

  1. HOW TO ORDER

    • Pick Up

      • Cakes are available in-stores. We recommend you contact the branch via landline or mobile to check stock availability.

    • Deliver

      • The following branches provide in-house delivery services:

      • Divisoria
      • Cogon
      • Jr. Borja
      • Corrales St.
      • CM. Recto Ave

      Delivery charge is Php 125 flat rate. The maximum radius for delivery is 5km from any of the branches above.


  2. PAYMENT METHODS

    • For in-store purchases (take-out)
      • Cash
      • Credit Card
      • GCash

    • For delivery transactions
      • Cash
      • GCash

    • For website transactions
      • PayPal
      • GCash
      • Bank transfer


  3. DELIVERY FEES

    4. Delivery fees will vary depending on the distance from delivering store:

    • Within 3km: Php 60
    • 3km - 5km: Php 85
    • 5km - 7km: Php 100
    • Beyond 7k within delivery scope: Php 150


  4. MINIMUM & MAXIMUM ORDER AMOUNT FOR DELIVERY

    • Minimum purchase: Php 750
    • Maximum purchase: 1 rider = 4 cakes at a time


  5. SIZE OF CAKES

    • Diameter: 8 inches
    • Height: 3 - 4 inches


  6. ADVANCED ORDERS

    7. All cake reservations and advanced orders must be paid for upon ordering. You may order at least a day in advance, so we can check stock availability and provide you with a new batch.   7. CAKE SHELF LIFE

    8. If stored properly:

    • Refrigerator Cakes: Maximum of 1 week
    • Freezer Cakes: Maximum of 1 week/li>

    We recommend that you consume the cake immediately. To prolong quality of cake when stored after being sliced: please cover sliced part with cling wrap or foil.
